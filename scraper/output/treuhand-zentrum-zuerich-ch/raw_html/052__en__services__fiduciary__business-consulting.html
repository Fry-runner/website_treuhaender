What types of company succession are there?

In Switzerland, the following forms of company succession are mainly distinguished:
  • Family-internal succession: Transfer to children or relatives
  • Management Buy-out (MBO): Takeover by existing management
  • Management Buy-in (MBI): Takeover by external management
  • Sale to third parties: External buyer, e.g., investor or competitor
  • Foundation model: Transfer to a non-profit or company-related foundation
