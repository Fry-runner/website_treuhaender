What is meant by startup?

A startup is a young, growth-oriented company that pursues an innovative business idea and is still in the build-up or development phase. Startups are often characterized by high growth potential, a scalable idea, and a certain financial risk.

What is the difference between a startup and a company?

The main difference lies in the phase and orientation:
  • A startup is in the initial phase, searches for a viable business model, and often focuses on innovation and rapid growth.
  • A company can already be established, pursues a stable business model, and is solidified in its structure and organization.
